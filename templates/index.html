Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our solution

How accurate is the AI detection?

Our AI models achieve 99.7% accuracy in detecting malicious code, with continuous improvements through machine learning. We maintain extremely low false positive rates (less than 0.5%) to minimize disruption to your workflow.

Which programming languages are supported?

We support 25+ programming languages including Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, C#, PHP, Ruby, Go, Rust, and more. Our AI models are continuously trained to recognize patterns across different languages.

Can I integrate this with my existing tools?

Yes! We provide plugins for popular IDEs (VS Code, IntelliJ, PyCharm), CI/CD platforms (Jenkins, GitLab, GitHub Actions), and a comprehensive REST API for custom integrations.

How long does a scan take?

Most scans complete in under 10 milliseconds per file. Large codebases are processed in parallel, with typical project scans completing in seconds rather than minutes.

Is my code kept private and secure?

Absolutely. All code is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never store your source code permanently, and analysis happens in isolated environments. Enterprise plans offer on-premise deployment options.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide email support for all plans, priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 dedicated support with custom SLAs for Enterprise customers. Our team includes security experts who can help with remediation strategies.
